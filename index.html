Acids, Bases & Indicators – Interactive Simulation

Acid: Substances which produce H+ ions in aqueous solution and have pH less than 7.
Base: Substances which produce OH− ions in aqueous solution and have pH greater than 7.
Indicator: Substances that change colour in acidic or basic medium and help us identify acids and bases.
